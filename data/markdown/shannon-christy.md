  * ## Overview
Dr. Christy's lab aims to promote cancer prevention and early detection behaviors (e.g., colorectal cancer screening, HPV vaccination) in order to reduce cancer incidence and mortality. 
### Associations

**Fellowship:**
    * Moffitt Cancer Center and Research Institute - Behavioral Oncology
  * ## Research Interest
Dr. Christy's research examines the associations between psychosocial factors, health beliefs, affect, healthcare access, patient-provider communication, and engagement in cancer preventive and early detection behaviors. Much of her research also involves evaluating the efficacy of behavioral interventions aimed at promoting cancer preventive and early detection behaviors and community and clinical collaborations.
