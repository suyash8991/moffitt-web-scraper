  * ## Research Interest
Dr. Fernandez's research focuses on developing cell-based therapies for acute myeloid leukemia, including the development of new transplant conditioning regimens for myeloid leukemias. He also works in the development of vaccine therapy pre- and post-transplantation for myeloid and lymphoid disorders.
