  * ## Research Interest
Dr. Brohl's research focus is in translational genomics, applying next-generation sequencing and assay technology to advance biologic discovery, identify novel targets for therapy, and assist clinical management. Recent projects include genomic landscape discovery in sarcoma subtypes, identification of drug targets from high-throughput “omics” datatypes, and development of genomics-based personalized cancer therapy program.
