  * ## Overview
Dr Alishahi is a physician, implementation scientist, and health services researcher. His research centers on developing, implementing and evaluating interventions to reduce unplanned emergency department visits and hospital admissions among patients with cancer.
### Associations

    * Gastrointestinal Oncology

  * ## Research Interest
1-Development of machine-learning modeling to predict 30-day unplanned hospital readmissions in adult patients with Gastrointestinal cancers. 2-Assess the effectiveness, feasibility, appropriateness, and acceptability of a remote symptom monitoring application among adult patients with cancer.3-Assess safety and efficacy of flexibly dosed esketamine nasal spray on depression and suicidality among diverse racial and ethnic patients with moderately to severely depression with pancreatic cancer.
