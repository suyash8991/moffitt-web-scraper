  * ## Research Interest
Deborah Cragun conducts research related to the following overarching aims:1) Assess and improve appropriate access to cancer genetic risk assessment and testing services2) Evaluate efficiency and outcomes of cancer genetic service delivery models3) Disseminate and implement evidence-based programs to prevent hereditary cancers.
