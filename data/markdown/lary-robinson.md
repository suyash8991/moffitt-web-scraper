  * ## Overview
Dr. Robinson specializes in the early detection of lung cancer and evaluation of indeterminant lung nodules. He is currently carrying out a clinical trial evaluation of blood biomarkers to diagnosis early-stage lung cancer.

  * ## Research Interest
Nutritional prehabilitation of patients with advanced lung cancer prior to immunotherapy using fermented foods and alsoin another study using a keto diet. He is evaluating the potential risk factors for the development of lung cancer in never smoking females. Finally, he is evaluating blood biomarkers for lung cancer early detection.
