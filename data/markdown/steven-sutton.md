  * ## Research Interest
Dr. Sutton’s primary research interests as a behavior scientist are individual differences in (1) cancer prevention behaviors and responses to interventions and (2) quality of life in cancer survivors. Quantitative issues of greatest interest to Dr. Sutton focus on behavior measurement, longitudinal data analysis and management of missing data using multiple imputations.
