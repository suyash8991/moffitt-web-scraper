  * ## Overview
Dr. Vinci's research focuses on developing behavioral interventions that address cancer-risk behaviors (e.g., tobacco, alcohol use) and stress management among cancer caregivers. To date, most of the interventional research she has conducted is specific to mindfulness-based interventions and strategies, with a primary focus on technology as a delivery modality.

  * ## Research Interest
Dr. Vinci has expertise in the following areas: 1) mindfulness-based interventions for cancer-risk behaviors; 2) technology as integrated into smoking cessation treatment; 3) cue reactivity among those who smoke and drink alcohol; 4) substance use among underserved populations; and 5) mindfulness-based interventions for cancer caregivers.
