  * ## Research Interest
Dr. Alsina is a specialist in multiple myeloma and bone marrow transplantation and cellular immunotherapy. Her clinical research focuses on the evaluation of novel drugs and combinations to treat myeloma and target minimal residual disease. She is the principal investigator of multiple myeloma studies focusing on maintenance therapy both post transplants;ant and post CART and novel immunotherapies in the relapsed and newly diagnosed settings. Dr. Alsina has served as a member of NCCN Myeloma Panel and the NCI Myeloma Steering Committee, as well as professional organizations such as the International Myeloma Society and the American Society of Hematology. She is actively involved in organizing educational programs for myeloma patients and co-chairs the Annual Hematologic Malignancies Program.
