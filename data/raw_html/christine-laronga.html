    • Research Interest

      Dr. Laronga's research involves creating proteomic profiles for different aspects of breast diseases (race; benign vs. cancer; genetic vs. sporadic breast cancer; etc.) Patient care revolves around diagnosing and treating breast cancer in a compassionate and cutting edge manner.
