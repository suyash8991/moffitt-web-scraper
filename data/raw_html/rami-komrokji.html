  • Dr. Komrokji’s research interests are in Phase I and II Clinical Trials, and in outcome research in hematologic malignancies with focus on myeloid neoplasms. His clinical interests are Myelodysplastic syndromes (MDS), Acute Leukemias, and Myeloproliferative neoplasms.
