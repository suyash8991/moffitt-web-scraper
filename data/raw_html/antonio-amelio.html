Antonio Amelio, PhD

Antonio Amelio, PhD

Vice Chair of Research, Head and Neck Oncology

Program: Tumor Microenvironment and Metastasis

Research Program: Cancer Biology & Evolution Program

View Lab Page

Contact

  • Overview

    The Amelio Lab is a cancer cell biology lab focused on discovering the molecular mechanisms that drive tumor development and progression.

    Associations

    • Tumor Microenvironment and Metastasis
    • Head and Neck-Endocrine Oncology
    • Cancer Biology & Evolution Program

  • Research Interest

    We use an interdisciplinary approach that integrates a combination of bioinformatics, genetically engineered cell line and animal models, pharmacologic and molecular genetic analyses, and synthetic biology approaches to investigate tumor development and progression and identify therapeutic vulnerabilities.
