    • Dr. Richard Kim specializes in gastrointestinal oncology. As a clinical investigator, Dr. Kim’s main interest is conducting novel clinical trials. Currently he is a principal investigator in multiple investigator-initiated and pharmaceutical phase I and phase II trials using novel targeted agents in gastrointestinal tumors, especially focusing on hepatobiliary and colon cancer.
